Pelmeni

Description

Pelmeni are traditional Russian dumplings consisting of a thin dough filled with a mixture of minced meat, which can include pork, beef, lamb, or chicken. They are typically boiled until tender and served with a dollop of sour cream, butter, or vinegar, often garnished with fresh herbs like dill.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water, or more as needed
  • 3 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour for dusting

Filling:

  • 18 ounces ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ice cold water
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
